Objective

Immediate continuity. Structured rollout. Smooth transition.

active value will provide immediate operational support to ensure continuity after the current transition and to support OQEMA's accelerated country website rollout to Ibexa DXP. Kaliop remains the technical implementation partner. active value acts as the central coordination, stakeholder management and editorial operations partner from the week of 14 April onwards.

Country status as of April 2026

We know where each market stands

CH and UK are currently rolling out. ES and FR content collected, rollout expected shortly. SE follows DK template, content expected imminently. BNL on track for April 24. BG and IT status unclear. CZ is a special case, on hold until product search is ready.

Role delineation

Clear boundaries from day one

Kaliop: technical implementation, development and technical troubleshooting. active value: rollout coordination, stakeholder management, content validation, Ibexa DXP editorial operations and final check support. OQEMA: final content approvals, legal pages and market decisions. Clear interfaces from day one.

Scope of Work

Two priorities, one team

Our scope reflects the two-phase structure of the GOWEB programme. Priority 1 is fully operational by June 15. Priority 2 begins immediately after as ongoing retainer engagement.

Priority 1

Stakeholder Management

  • Direct communication with local editors across all markets
  • Content deadline follow-up and status reporting
  • Q&A sessions with local teams
  • Escalation management to OQEMA Group Marketing
  • Coordination interface with Kaliop
Priority 1

Editorial Operations

  • Review and validation of locally collected content
  • Definition of localisation requirements per market
  • Upload of approved content to Ibexa DXP (EN version)
  • Quality check before automated translation
  • Support for final local review before launch
Priority 1

Rollout Coordination

  • Country-by-country tracking against master timeline
  • Coordination of "under construction" fallback for late markets
  • Onboarding new local editors to Ibexa DXP workflow
  • Compliance support: Usercentrics and eTracker alignment
Priority 2

Ongoing Platform Development

  • UX/UI optimisation and content improvements
  • Analytics setup and eTracker operational support
  • Coordination of new feature requirements with Kaliop
  • Continuous editorial operations for platform maintenance
  • Process improvement across markets

Commercial Approach

Flexible by design. Capped for clarity.

Given the varying level of country readiness and the evolving scope of the rollout, active value proposes a Time & Material model with a mutually agreed budget cap rather than a fixed-price model. This gives OQEMA full flexibility while providing cost predictability.
